# E-Commerce Customer Review Analysis Dashboard

### **Blinkit vs Zepto vs JioMart | Power BI Data Analytics Project**

---

# 📝 Short Description

This project analyzes customer reviews collected from three leading Indian quick-commerce platforms—**Blinkit, Zepto, and JioMart**. Using **Power BI**, the dashboard compares review volume, average ratings, customer sentiment, review trends, and frequently used positive and negative words to understand customer satisfaction across platforms.

The dashboard helps identify which platform delivers the best customer experience, where customers face the most issues, and what improvements businesses should prioritize.

---

# 🎯 Project Purpose

The objective of this project is to transform raw customer review data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Compare overall performance of Blinkit, Zepto, and JioMart
* Understand customer sentiment
* Identify strengths and weaknesses of each platform
* Monitor review trends over time
* Discover common topics in positive and negative reviews
* Recommend business improvements based on customer feedback

---

# 🛠 Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following technologies:

* **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and visualization
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs
* **Microsoft Excel** – Source dataset
* **Kaggle Dataset** – Customer review data

---

**Source:** Kaggle

The dataset contains customer reviews collected from three major Indian quick-commerce platforms:

* Blinkit

# ⭐ Dashboard Features & Highlights

## 📌 Business Problem

Online grocery and quick-commerce companies receive thousands of customer reviews every month. Manually analyzing this feedback is difficult and time-consuming, making it challenging to identify customer pain points, compare competitors, and improve service quality.

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

* Compare customer satisfaction across platforms
* Analyze review volume and average ratings
* Measure positive, neutral, and negative sentiment
* Identify common customer complaints
* Track review trends over time
* Support strategic business decisions using customer feedback

---

# 📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of overall platform performance.

### KPIs Displayed

* Total Reviews
* Average Rating
* Positive Reviews
* Negative Reviews
* Positive Review Percentage
* Negative Review Percentage

### Visuals

* Platform-wise Average Rating
* Review Volume by Platform
* Overall Sentiment Distribution
* Platform, Year, and Quarter Filters

### Key Findings

* Blinkit records the highest average customer rating.
* Zepto has the highest number of customer reviews.
* Negative reviews dominate overall customer feedback.
* Positive reviews represent only a small percentage of total reviews.

---

## 2️⃣ Platform Comparison

This page compares the three platforms side by side.

### Visuals

* Review Volume per Platform
* Average Rating Comparison
* Rating Distribution (1–5 Stars)

### Insights

* Zepto receives the highest review volume.
* Blinkit consistently achieves the highest average ratings.
* JioMart records the lowest customer satisfaction.
* Higher review volume does not necessarily indicate better customer experience.

---

## 3️⃣ Trend Analysis

This page analyzes customer feedback over time.

### Visuals

* Monthly Review Volume Trend
* Monthly Rating Trends by Platform

### Insights

* Review activity remained relatively low until mid-2024.
* Customer reviews increased significantly during late 2024.
* Blinkit maintained stronger rating performance compared to competitors.
* Most monthly average ratings remained below 3 stars, indicating overall customer dissatisfaction.

---

## 4️⃣ Sentiment Analysis

This page focuses on customer opinion and text analysis.

### Visuals

* Sentiment Distribution
* Sentiment Comparison by Platform
* Top Positive Words
* Top Negative Words

### Insights

* Nearly 80% of customer reviews are negative.
* Blinkit has the highest share of positive reviews.
* Delivery-related issues appear most frequently in negative feedback.
* Positive reviews commonly mention good service, fast delivery, and quality.

---

## 5️⃣ Executive Summary

The final page summarizes overall findings and business recommendations.

### Summary Cards

* Best Performing Platform
* Platform Needing Improvement
* Overall Average Rating

### Business Recommendations

* Improve delivery speed and logistics planning.
* Reduce recurring customer complaints.
* Strengthen refund and customer support processes.
* Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews.
* Benchmark Blinkit's customer experience strategies.

### SWOT Analysis

**Strength**

* Blinkit maintains the highest customer ratings.

**Weakness**

* Negative reviews dominate across all platforms.

**Opportunity**

* Improve logistics, customer service, and delivery experience.

**Threat**

* Persistent negative feedback may reduce customer loyalty and impact brand reputation.

---

# 💡 Business Impact & Insights

This dashboard provides valuable insights for business leaders and decision-makers by:

* Identifying the platform with the strongest customer satisfaction.
* Highlighting major customer pain points through sentiment analysis.
* Revealing common themes in positive and negative feedback.
* Tracking changes in customer engagement over time.
* Supporting operational improvements using data-driven recommendations.
* Helping businesses prioritize customer experience initiatives based on real customer feedback.

---

# 📈 Key Insights

* Blinkit achieved the highest average customer rating among all platforms.
* Zepto generated the highest review volume.
* JioMart showed the lowest overall customer satisfaction.
* Approximately 80% of customer reviews were negative.
* Delivery, service quality, and refund issues were the most common customer complaints.
* Positive reviews primarily highlighted fast delivery, good service, and product quality.
* Review volume increased sharply during late 2024, indicating higher customer engagement.
